<p><ul><li>OCA</li><li>OCR</li><li>SCA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Full-lamination Optical Film market encompasses several types: Optical Clear Adhesive (OCA), Optical Clear Resin (OCR), and Surface Control Adhesive (SCA). OCA is primarily used for bonding displays due to its high transparency and adhesion properties. OCR serves to enhance optical performance while adding durability to displays. SCA focuses on improving surface properties, such as scratch resistance and anti-reflection. Together, these materials are essential for optimizing display quality in smartphones, tablets, and other electronic devices.</p></p>
