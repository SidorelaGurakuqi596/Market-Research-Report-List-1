<p><p>The Sealed Maintenance Free (SMF) battery market primarily features two key types: Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) batteries and Gel batteries. AGM batteries utilize a fiberglass mat to absorb the electrolyte, providing enhanced durability and vibration resistance, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Gel batteries, on the other hand, contain a gelled electrolyte, which minimizes the risk of spillage and enables a slower discharge rate. Both types are favored for their maintenance-free operation, safety, and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
