<p><p>The Automatic COD Analysers market is segmented into various types based on the methods used to measure Chemical Oxygen Demand (COD). The Potassium Dichromate Method is a widely adopted technique that uses dichromate to oxidize organic matter, providing accurate COD measurements. The Potassium Permanganate Method employs permanganate as a strong oxidizing agent, suitable for detecting lower levels of COD in specific samples. Other methods may include advanced techniques such as photometric or electrochemical approaches, catering to diverse analytical needs.</p></p>
