<p><p>The digital glossmeters market comprises three main types: three-angle glossmeters, dual-angle glossmeters, and single-angle glossmeters. Three-angle glossmeters measure gloss at multiple angles, providing comprehensive surface analysis for various materials. Dual-angle glossmeters offer measurements at two fixed angles, balancing detail with usability for assessing gloss levels. Single-angle glossmeters focus on measuring gloss at a single angle, making them suitable for specific applications where simplicity is key. Each type serves distinct industries, catering to diverse quality control and inspection needs.</p></p>
