<p><strong>The Elevator Proximity Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-beam Door Sensors</li><li>Ultrasonic Door Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Elevator proximity sensors enhance safety and efficiency by detecting the presence of passengers near doors. Multi-beam door sensors utilize multiple infrared beams to create an invisible barrier, ensuring doors remain open if individuals are detected, preventing accidents. Ultrasonic door sensors operate by emitting sound waves that bounce off nearby objects, identifying movement and preventing door closure when passengers are present. Both types contribute to smoother elevator operations and increased safety by minimizing the risk of entrapment or injury.</p></p>
