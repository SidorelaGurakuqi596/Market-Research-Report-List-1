High voltage tape, on the other hand, is engineered for environments with elevated voltage levels, ensuring superior electrical insulation and safety. Both types utilize a pressure-sensitive adhesive for easy application and maintenance, catering to diverse industrial and commercial needs in electrical insulation solutions.</p></p>
