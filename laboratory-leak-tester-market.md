<p><ul><li>Hammering Method</li><li>Ultrasound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laboratory Leak Tester market encompasses various methods to detect leaks in systems and products. The Hammering Method involves striking an object to create pressure changes that reveal leaks. In contrast, the Ultrasound method employs high-frequency sound waves to identify leak sources by detecting escaping gases or fluids. Both methods offer distinct advantages, with the Hammering Method being straightforward and cost-effective, while Ultrasound provides precision and the ability to detect leaks in challenging environments, enhancing testing efficiency across industries.</p></p>
