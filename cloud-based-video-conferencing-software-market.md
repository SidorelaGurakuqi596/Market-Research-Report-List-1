<p><strong>The Cloud Based Video Conferencing Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SVC Architecture</li><li>AVC Architecture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud-based video conferencing software operates primarily through two architectures: SVC (Scalable Video Coding) and AVC (Advanced Video Coding). SVC architecture allows for adaptive video quality, enabling seamless adjustments based on network conditions, which enhances user experience during variable bandwidth scenarios. In contrast, AVC architecture provides high-quality video at a fixed bitrate, suitable for stable connections. Both architectures cater to diverse user needs, with SVC offering flexibility for dynamic environments and AVC delivering consistent quality in controlled settings.</p></p>
