<p><p>The Peritoneal Dialysis Equipment Market primarily includes two types: Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis (CAPD) and Automated Peritoneal Dialysis (APD). CAPD involves manual infusion and drainage of dialysis solutions at regular intervals throughout the day, allowing for patient independence and flexibility. In contrast, APD utilizes a machine to automate the process, typically performed overnight, offering convenience and efficiency. Both methods aim to effectively remove waste products and excess fluid, catering to diverse patient needs in renal care.</p></p>
