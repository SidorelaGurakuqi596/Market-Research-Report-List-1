<p><p>On-Premise Video Conferencing Systems are categorized into two primary types: Multipoint Systems and Endpoint Systems. Multipoint Systems facilitate communication among multiple users simultaneously, allowing for larger group meetings and conferences. They typically require a dedicated server to manage video streams and ensure optimal quality. Endpoint Systems, on the other hand, focus on individual or small-group connections and are designed for direct communication with other endpoints or systems. Both types provide secure, high-quality video conferencing solutions suitable for enterprise environments.</p></p>
