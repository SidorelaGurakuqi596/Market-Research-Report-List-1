<p><strong>The Polyimide Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastics</li><li>Thermosetting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyimide resins market encompasses two primary types: thermoplastics and thermosetting resins. Thermoplastic polyimides are characterized by their ability to be reshaped upon heating, offering flexibility and ease of processing, making them suitable for applications requiring high-temperature resistance and mechanical strength. In contrast, thermosetting polyimides cure into a rigid structure when heated, providing excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance, ideal for aerospace, electronics, and automotive industries. Both types are essential for advanced materials in demanding environments.</p></p>
