<p><p>The Acute Ischemic Stroke (AIS) market comprises three primary therapeutic categories: Antihypertensive therapeutics, Antiplatelet therapeutics, and Anticoagulants. Antihypertensive agents are crucial for managing elevated blood pressure to prevent secondary strokes. Antiplatelet therapeutics inhibit platelet aggregation, reducing thrombus formation and improving blood flow. Anticoagulants play a role in preventing further clot development by inhibiting the coagulation cascade. These therapeutic categories are essential in the management of AIS, aiming to optimize patient outcomes and reduce long-term disabilities.</p></p>
