<p><p>The Aqueous Polyurethane Resins market consists of two primary types: Aqueous Polyurethane Dispersion (APD) and Aqueous Polyurethane Emulsion (APE). APD offers excellent performance in coatings, adhesives, and sealants with high stability and low VOC emissions. It provides a uniform film formation and enhanced durability. APE, on the other hand, is characterized by smaller particle size and lower viscosity, making it suitable for applications requiring better flexibility and high gloss finishes. Both types are eco-friendly alternatives to solvent-based resins.</p></p>
