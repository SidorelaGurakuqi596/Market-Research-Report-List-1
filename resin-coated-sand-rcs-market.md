<p><p>Resin-coated Sand (RCS) is a specialized material used primarily in the foundry industry for metal casting processes. It involves sand grains coated with a thermosetting resin that provides enhanced properties such as reduced moisture absorption, improved flowability, and better strength at elevated temperatures. These characteristics lead to better casting performance, dimensional accuracy, and surface finish.</p><p>The RCS market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the rising demand for precision-engineered components in automotive, aerospace, and construction sectors.
