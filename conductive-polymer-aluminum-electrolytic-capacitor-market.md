Radial Lead Type features leads protruding from one end, making them easy to mount in through-hole designs. SMD Type capacitors are designed for surface mount technology, allowing for automated assembly and saving space on printed circuit boards. Each type serves distinct applications based on size, mounting method, and performance requirements.</p></p>
